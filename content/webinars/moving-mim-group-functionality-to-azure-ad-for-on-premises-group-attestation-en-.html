Moving MIM Group Functionality to Azure AD

MajorKey Technologies Webinar MIM Migration Group Management Access Reviews

Summary

Covers how to replace MIM-based group management and attestation with Entra ID native capabilities. Focuses on dynamic groups, group-based access reviews, and self-service group management as replacements for MIM group sync and approval workflows.

Key Takeaways

  • MIM group management can be largely replaced by Entra ID dynamic membership rules based on user attributes
  • Group-based access reviews in Entra ID Governance replace MIM-based attestation workflows for periodic group membership validation
  • Self-service group management with approval workflows reduces IT burden and empowers business owners to manage their own groups
  • Nested group scenarios and complex membership calculations may require HorizonID or custom logic beyond native Entra capabilities
  • On-premises group writeback from Entra ID to AD replaces MIM-based group sync for hybrid environments
