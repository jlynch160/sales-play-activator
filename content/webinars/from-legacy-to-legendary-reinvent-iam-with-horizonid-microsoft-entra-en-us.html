From Legacy to Legendary - Reinvent IAM with HorizonID & Microsoft Entra

MajorKey Technologies Webinar HorizonID Identity Governance Entra Migration

Summary

MajorKey presents HorizonID, their identity governance accelerator built on top of Microsoft Entra ID Governance. The session covers how HorizonID solves gaps in native Entra governance - particularly around complex provisioning, application integration, and joiner/mover/leaver automation. Includes customer case studies and deployment timelines.

Key Takeaways

  • HorizonID extends Microsoft Entra ID Governance to handle complex provisioning scenarios that native Entra cannot solve out of the box
  • The platform automates joiner/mover/leaver workflows including application-specific provisioning logic for ServiceNow, Workday, SAP, and custom apps
  • Organizations running legacy IGA platforms (SailPoint IIQ, Oracle IAM, One Identity) can migrate to Entra + HorizonID without losing automation capabilities
  • Typical deployment timeline is 8-12 weeks for core governance versus 6-12 months for legacy IGA platforms
  • HorizonID includes pre-built connectors and workflow templates that eliminate the custom development required by most IGA deployments
