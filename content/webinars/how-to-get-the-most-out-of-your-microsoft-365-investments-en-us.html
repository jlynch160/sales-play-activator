Get the Most Out of Your Microsoft 365 Investments

MajorKey Technologies Webinar Microsoft 365 License Optimization E5 Features

Summary

Examines how organizations can maximize the identity and security capabilities already included in their Microsoft 365 and E5 licenses. Covers underutilized features in Entra ID, Defender, Intune, and Purview that most organizations are paying for but not using.

Key Takeaways

  • Most organizations with E5 licenses are only using 30-40% of the identity and security capabilities they are already paying for
  • Entra ID P2 features like PIM, access reviews, and identity protection are included in E5 but often not deployed
  • Microsoft Defender XDR capabilities for identity threat detection are included but require configuration to provide value
  • Intune compliance policies feed directly into conditional access - combining them creates a zero trust device posture check at no additional cost
  • A phased enablement approach starting with the highest-risk users and expanding outward delivers quick wins and builds internal support
