All Access is Privileged - Taking a PAM-First Approach

MajorKey Technologies Webinar Privileged Access Management Identity Convergence Non-Human Identity

Summary

MajorKey explores why every identity should be treated as privileged, not just domain admins and root accounts. The session covers the expanding scope of privileged access to include ERP admins, cloud tenant admins, third-party vendors, non-human identities, and DevOps service accounts. The webinar reviews the PAM vendor landscape including CyberArk, Delinea, BeyondTrust, and Netrix, and explains how PAM converges with IGA and app governance for a unified identity security program.

Key Takeaways

  • Privileged access extends far beyond domain admins - includes ERP admins, cloud tenant admins, third-party vendors, endpoint admins, and non-human identities
  • The PAM vendor landscape is converging: CyberArk acquired Venafi for machine identity, Delinea acquired Fastpath for IGA, showing platforms are merging PAM with governance
  • Just-in-time access enforcement through PAM reduces standing privileges and limits blast radius of compromised accounts
  • Integration between PAM and UEBA/XDR is critical for detecting anomalous privileged sessions and stopping lateral movement
  • Organizations need to bring DevOps secrets, API tokens, and service account credentials into their PAM program - not just human admin accounts
