Demonstrate Success and Grow the ROI of CIAM Programs

MajorKey Technologies Webinar CIAM Customer Identity ROI Measurement

Summary

Focused on customer identity and access management (CIAM) - how to measure the business impact of CIAM investments and demonstrate ROI to stakeholders. Covers key metrics, customer experience improvements, fraud reduction, and strategies for growing CIAM program maturity.

Key Takeaways

  • CIAM ROI should be measured across three dimensions: security (fraud reduction), experience (conversion rates), and compliance (data privacy)
  • Customer friction during authentication directly impacts revenue - every additional step in login reduces conversion by measurable percentages
  • Progressive profiling collects customer data gradually rather than requiring full registration upfront, improving completion rates
  • CIAM platforms should unify customer identity across channels (web, mobile, in-store) to provide consistent security and experience
  • Demonstrating CIAM success requires baseline metrics before implementation and ongoing measurement of registration rates, login success, and fraud incidents
