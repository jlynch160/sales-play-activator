Machine Identities - Uncovering Hidden Risks in Your Environment

MajorKey Technologies Webinar Machine Identity Non-Human Identity Secret Management

Summary

Explores the growing risk of ungoverned machine identities - service accounts, API keys, certificates, and bot accounts that operate without human oversight. Covers the scale of the problem (machine identities outnumber human identities 10-40x), discovery challenges, and governance strategies.

Key Takeaways

  • Machine identities outnumber human identities by 10-40x in most enterprises, yet less than 20% of organizations actively govern them
  • Service accounts with static passwords that never rotate are the most common machine identity risk - they persist indefinitely with elevated access
  • API keys and secrets hardcoded in source code repositories are regularly discovered by attackers through automated scanning
  • Certificate expiration causes outages - organizations need automated lifecycle management to prevent unplanned downtime
  • A machine identity governance program should start with discovery and inventory before attempting to enforce policies on what you cannot see
