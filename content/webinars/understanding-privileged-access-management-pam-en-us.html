Understanding Privileged Access Management (PAM)

MajorKey Technologies Webinar PAM Fundamentals Credential Vaulting Just-in-Time Access

Summary

A comprehensive introduction to PAM covering what constitutes privileged access, why it matters, the major PAM platforms, and how to build a PAM program from discovery through full deployment. Includes practical guidance on vaulting, session management, and just-in-time access.

Key Takeaways

  • Privileged accounts are involved in nearly every major breach - controlling them is the single highest-impact security investment
  • PAM programs should start with discovery and vaulting of known privileged accounts before expanding to session monitoring and JIT access
  • CyberArk and BeyondTrust remain the dominant PAM platforms, each with different architectural approaches and strengths
  • Just-in-time (JIT) privileged access eliminates standing admin accounts by granting temporary elevation only when needed, with full audit trails
  • PAM integration with ITSM (ServiceNow, Jira) enables approval workflows that tie privileged access to change tickets, ensuring accountability
