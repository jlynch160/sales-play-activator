Unmasking Invisible Threats - Securing Non-Human Identities

MajorKey Technologies Webinar Non-Human Identity Service Account Risk Identity Discovery

Summary

Examines the security risks posed by non-human identities (service accounts, API keys, bots, managed identities) and strategies for bringing them under governance. Covers discovery techniques, risk assessment, and the operational challenge of governing identities that have no human owner.

Key Takeaways

  • Non-human identities are invisible to most security programs because they do not authenticate interactively and are not managed through HR processes
  • Orphaned service accounts - those whose creator has left the organization - are the highest-risk non-human identities because no one monitors them
  • Machine identity sprawl accelerates with cloud adoption as every microservice, container, and serverless function creates new identities
  • Governance for non-human identities requires assigning human owners who are accountable for the identity's access and credential hygiene
  • Automated discovery tools are essential because manual inventory of machine identities is impossible at cloud scale
