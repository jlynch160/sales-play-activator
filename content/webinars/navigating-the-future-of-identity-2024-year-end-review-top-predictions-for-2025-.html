Navigating the Future of Identity - 2024 Review & 2025 Predictions

MajorKey Technologies Webinar Identity Trends AI Threats 2025 Predictions

Summary

MajorKey's year-end review of identity security trends from 2024 and predictions for 2025. Covers the rise of AI-driven identity threats, the convergence of identity platforms, machine identity governance, and the shift toward continuous access evaluation.

Key Takeaways

  • AI-driven identity threats (deepfakes, automated social engineering) are the fastest-growing attack category heading into 2025
  • Platform convergence continues - PAM, IGA, and workforce authentication are merging into unified identity security platforms
  • Machine identity governance will move from emerging to essential as organizations discover the scale of their non-human identity sprawl
  • Continuous access evaluation will replace session-based authentication - real-time risk signals will trigger re-authentication mid-session
  • Identity programs that demonstrate measurable business outcomes (breach prevention, compliance cost reduction) will receive sustained investment
