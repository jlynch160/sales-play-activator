Powering Business Resilience Through Identity Security

MajorKey Technologies Webinar Business Resilience Managed Services Identity Operations

Summary

Covers how identity security directly enables business resilience and continuity. Discusses the connection between identity controls and operational uptime, the role of managed identity services in maintaining security posture, and how service management practices apply to identity operations.

Key Takeaways

  • Identity infrastructure is a business-critical service - an Entra ID outage or a compromised privileged account can halt operations across the enterprise
  • Managed identity operations (L2/L3 support) ensure continuous monitoring, patching, and incident response for identity systems
  • Service management practices (change management, incident response, SLAs) should be applied to identity infrastructure just like core IT services
  • Business resilience planning must include identity failover scenarios - what happens when your primary IdP is unavailable
  • Identity security metrics (mean time to detect compromised accounts, mean time to revoke access) directly correlate with business resilience
